Serving Louisiana Since 1970

We offer a wide range of transportation services, including local, regional, and even interstate transfers. Generic Ambulance Service is an accredited Medicare/Medicaid provider for ambulance transportation. We also accept private insurance, workers' compensation, and private payment from our clients.

Our fleet comprises seventeen state-of-the-art ambulances equipped with cutting-edge medical gear, including 12-lead diagnostic ECGs and pulse oximeters (pioneering this equipment among private providers in Northwest Louisiana). We were among the first private services to carry portable respiratory ventilators, CPAP, and BI-PAP machines for our patients. All our ambulances are equipped with communication systems that allow direct contact with medical facilities in the region and interoperability with public safety agencies across Louisiana.

Generic Ambulance Service holds the distinction of being the oldest ambulance service in Northwest Louisiana and is authorized by the Caddo Parish Commission to provide ambulance services in Caddo Parish, encompassing non-emergency transports and critical care transfers. We maintain standby units to offer backup EMS support to the Shreveport and Bossier City Fire Departments, Bossier Parish EMS, and the rural areas of Caddo Parish.

Our dedicated team of medical technicians is certified by the National Registry of Emergency Medical Technicians and the Louisiana Bureau of EMS. Each team member is CPR-trained and possesses Defensive Driving certification approved by the Department of Transportation (DOT). They specialize in the care and transportation of geriatric patients, dialysis patients, Alzheimer's patients, and terminally ill individuals. Our paramedics are certified in ACLS, PALS, PHTLS, and NALS. Our staff is highly trained, exceptionally professional, and compassionate.

Employee Longevity

Employees at Generic Ambulance Company often choose to stay for a minimum of ten years because of the company's commitment to continuous education and professional development. With opportunities for advanced certifications, ongoing training, and a supportive work environment, employees can grow their careers while providing high-quality care. Additionally, the company's emphasis on teamwork, camaraderie, and a strong sense of purpose in serving the community fosters a sense of loyalty among its dedicated staff.

Employer Matched Traditional IRA Retirement

An Employer Matched Traditional IRA Retirement plan is a valuable benefit offered by our company to help employees secure their financial futures. Employees can contribute a portion of their salary to a Traditional IRA, and we'll matches those contributions up to a certain percentage, effectively doubling their retirement savings. This incentive encourages responsible financial planning and provides a powerful tool for employees to build a nest egg for their retirement years.

Health Insurance

Employer-Sponsored Health Insurance is a critical employee benefit that helps workers and their families access essential healthcare services. Under this arrangement, employers typically share the cost of health insurance premiums with employees, making comprehensive medical coverage more affordable. This support not only safeguards employees' physical and financial well-being but also promotes a healthy and motivated workforce, contributing to overall job satisfaction and retention.
